Ethics in IT: Cybersecurity

With the growing cyber threats in our modern age such as malware a question is raised: How to balance security and privacy? Privacy is a given right that everyone is entitled to but everyone has to give up some level of privacy to ensure security for themselves as well as others. A major challenge arises of finding a balance between one's rights and their security. One of the major aspect of cybersecurity is one's right to privacy. This is important because a large part of cybersecurity is protecting people's data. Companies and institutions must be able to secure people's information while also making it accessible. This is an ongoing struggle which is depicted by the numerous data breaches that happen. As we look to the future it will be important to keep these ideas in mind.
